General Instructions

  • College ID is mandatory at the time of reporting
  • Online registrations will be opened till 09:30 PM on 12.03.2023
  • Event schedule will be shared with the participants through WhatsApp group
  • Registered participants must report at the event venue between 08:30 AM to 09:00 AM on 16.03.2023
  • Participants are requested to follow the schedule time strictly
  • Participation in technical events is must in order to participate in non-technical events
  • Each person is permitted to participate in only 2 technical and non-technical events as per their wish
  • Participation fee per head is Rs.150
  • Lunch will be provided for all participants
  • An additional amount of Rs.300 will be charged for accommodation

Paper Presentation - Rules & Regulation

  • Each team can have maximum of 3 Participants
  • Prelims will be based on abstract
  • Last Date for abstract submission will be intimated in whatsapp group
  • The Presentation can have maximum of 12 slides
  • Each presentation team will get 6 min for presentation + 03 min for Query Session
  • Topics can be anything related to computer science domain
